What do these mean? (icons, colors, terms)

Icons & colors: ✓ good · ⚠ warning · ✗ fail · — not applicable or no data · ★ clean kill (no wipes this week). Green/yellow/orange/red bands follow the same good→fail scale throughout.

Active%: share of the night's fight segments the player was present for. A low number means verify why (late join, disconnect, early leave) — it is not automatically misconduct.

  • Correction (2026-07-09) — the "Scorch/Sunder Violations" metric was miscounting. Fire Vulnerability (Improved Scorch) and Sunder Armor both decay after 30s and *have to* be periodically recast once at 5 stacks to keep the debuff from expiring — that maintenance recast is correct play, not a mistake. The metric was flagging every recast at 5 stacks as a "violation" regardless of how much duration was left on the debuff. `tools/raid-analyzer/src/rpb/violations.py` now only counts a recast as a violation when it landed with >10s still remaining (genuinely too early to be necessary). Corrected counts for tonight, after re-running with the fix:

  • Realblod: 3 (was 4) — negligible either way.
  • Treat the "recurring Jourganoff Scorch problem" noted in prior weeks' reports with the same skepticism until those are re-checked against the fixed metric.
  • Re-running also surfaced separately that three players' active% figures moved between two back-to-back full pulls of this same immutable report (Chopmedckoff 35%→45%, Skindog 60%→50%, Shadydoktor 70%→68%) — likely WCL-side data instability during a window where their API was also throwing 502/503s. The Summary Table and notes above already reflect the corrected (second-pull) numbers, but it's worth knowing tonight's report needed two independent full fetches to land on stable figures for some players.

What do these mean? (icons, colors, terms)

Icons & colors: ✓ good · ⚠ warning · ✗ fail · — not applicable or no data · ★ clean kill (no wipes this week). Green/yellow/orange/red bands follow the same good→fail scale throughout.

Avoidable Damage: damage taken from a mechanic the player could have avoided by moving or positioning differently (e.g. standing in Rain of Fire).

Active%: share of the night's fight segments the player was present for. A low number means verify why (late join, disconnect, early leave) — it is not automatically misconduct.

Consumes: weighted score across Flask, Battle elixir, Guardian elixir, and Food buff uptime for the night.

Boss short names: HKM = High King Maulgar · KT = Kael'thas Sunstrider · Leo = Leotheras the Blind · Solarian = High Astromancer Solarian · Karathress = Fathom-Lord Karathress.

Gear shorthand: "Aldor Hon" / "Scryer Hon" = Aldor/Scryer honored-tier reputation enchant (a cheaper tier than exalted) · "40SP" = a weapon enchant granting +40 spell power · "Mystic Thread" = a budget leg armor kit · "uncommon gem" = a green-quality (budget) gem instead of a rare/epic one.
